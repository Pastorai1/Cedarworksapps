PastorAI

For pastors & church staff

A suite of AI-powered tools that help pastors and church staff carry the week — sermon and series planning, Bible study, devotionals, children's ministry, social media, and translation, all gathered into one place.

Instead of juggling a dozen tabs and starting every task from a blank page, a pastor opens one tool and gets a running start on the work that fills a ministry week — then shapes it in their own voice.

Sales training

Ready Room

For reps & closers

A flight simulator for sales calls. Rehearse your pitch on an AI that plays a realistic, often skeptical prospect — one that talks back out loud and reacts to how you sell. Ask sharp, low-pressure questions and it opens up; push too hard and it shuts down, just like real life.

Drill the two skills that win deals: discovery — the questions that uncover what a prospect actually wants — and objection-handling on price, timing, and trust. Train at easy, medium, and hard levels, then get every session graded by an AI coach that shows what worked, what cost you the deal, and exactly what to fix next. Voice-first, with typing whenever you prefer.

The best closers aren't born. They're rehearsed.
